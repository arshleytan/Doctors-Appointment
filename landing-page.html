Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some Frequently Asked Questions in D'Appointment doctors.

  • Do you allow appointment in weekends?

    No. Our doctors need also a rest and time for theirselves. Weekends are their rest days for theirselves and for their families.

  • Do you accept credit card in payment?

    Yes. We accept credit card and some e-cash platform like Gcash for payment method.

  • Can we not follow the appointment incase of emergency?

    If the reason of appointment rescheduling is valid we allow it. But if not, no. Many patients setting an appointment so it's hard to make a rescheduling especially in not valid matters or reason.

  • Can we make a private chat with doctors?

    If your doctor gives his/her social media name to contact him/her, Yes. But be assured that the conversation is about health related problems.
